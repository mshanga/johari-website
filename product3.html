Johari Premium Loose Tea

Taste & Aroma: Our Tea is a blend of Kenya's premium teas, carefully hand-picked. It has a strong colour, rich taste and natural flavour form the East and West of the Kenyan highlands.

  • Description

Product Description

Jewels are normally found in the state of nature. Although processed and mined they retain their beauty. What’s more is, finding a jewel is not an easy process. One would have to mine in different areas, probably hit a dead end. At Johari we aim to provide you with the highest quality tea by cultivating the art of picking and homogenizing various tea types while retaining tea in its authentic state. Our team is equipped to handle tea maintaining health and hygiene standards while also having the right skills, knowledge and environment to create the right blend.

Our name not only represents the uniqueness of our taste but embraces the story of our country. Tea is the heart of Kenya. It is a way for people to connect and come together – over a cup of tea. We desire to create a personalized experience for everyone drinking Johari in order to bring more people together and create healthy lifestyle due to the anti-oxide effect contained in our tea.
