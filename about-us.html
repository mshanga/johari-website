The Mountain

At the pinnacle of Mt. Kenya is where our journey begins.

Stretching towards the East, lies lighter, brighter, toned tea leaves embodied with the freshest aromas. While to the West sits darker, bolder tea leaves belted with a rich and powerful taste. When East meets West – A harmonized balance of flavour, colour and scent is created.

Leaf to Jewel

Born out of this journey is Johari, which means Jewels in both Swahili and Arabic. Our name embraces the different element associated with our process. Upon first glance of a jewel, one is enchanted by its beauty and rarity. Similarly, our package is designed to captivate our clients and customers offering value and luxury – at an affordable price.
